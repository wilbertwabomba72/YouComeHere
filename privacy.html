Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how You Come Here handles information when you use the game. The game is designed for simple mobile play, but it may use security, advertising, and analytics-related services to protect the app, prevent abuse, and support optional advertising features.

Information We May Process

The app may process limited technical information, including:

  • Device identifiers such as Identifier for Vendor (IDFV)
  • App bundle identifier, app version, device model, operating system version, and language or region settings
  • Gameplay progress stored locally on the device, such as unlocked levels and stars
  • Advertising-related identifiers when permitted by the user through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework
  • Technical logs, security signals, crash-related diagnostics, and network request metadata

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Run core game features and save local progress
  • Protect the app, prevent fraud, and maintain service security
  • Load remote advertising configuration when available
  • Initialize and show rewarded ads after the App Tracking Transparency flow has completed
  • Improve stability, performance, and user experience

Advertising And Third-Party SDKs

The app may integrate advertising SDKs such as Chartboost and Yandex Mobile Ads. Rewarded ads are only requested when ad unit parameters are available from the remote configuration service. Advertising SDKs are initialized after the App Tracking Transparency flow is completed.

Depending on your privacy choices and the SDK provider's own policies, advertising partners may process device information, ad interactions, approximate technical information, and advertising identifiers where permitted.

App Tracking Transparency

On supported iOS versions, the app uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before using tracking-related advertising features. You can allow or deny tracking in the system prompt, and you can change your choice later in iOS Settings.

Security SDK

The app may use a security SDK to help detect abuse, maintain app integrity, and protect the service. The security SDK may use technical device and app information, including IDFV, for security and anti-fraud purposes.

Remote Configuration

The app may request remote advertising configuration from a server using the app bundle identifier. If no advertising configuration is returned, the app does not request rewarded ads.

Data Retention

Local gameplay progress remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear the app data. Third-party SDK providers may retain data according to their own policies and legal requirements.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer through the App Store product page so the issue can be reviewed.

Your Choices

You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also delete the app to remove locally stored game progress from your device.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the developer through the App Store product page for You Come Here.
